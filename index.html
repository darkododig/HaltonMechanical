Racking Repair & Warehouse Solutions

Damaged racking is a safety liability and an MOL red flag. Our team delivers certified repairs, column replacements, and rack reinforcements that bring your warehouse back into compliance and keep operations moving.

  • Rack damage assessment & reporting
  • Column & upright repair and replacement
  • Beam & frame reinforcement
  • Post-inspection remediation
  • New rack installation & reconfiguration
  • Bollard & protection guarding installation
